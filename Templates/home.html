Keep Track of Food

Using the Edamam Foodbase API you can pick out any food you eat and see details about it. You get to see its calories and macromolecue content compared to the total amount of calories and macromolecues you ate today. All it takes is a click of the plus button and you can search for your desired food. Then just add how much of it you ate and watch it get added to your total food eaten.




Track Your exercise

Search and select any exercise across a database of 850+ exercises. With a click of the plus button you can search for your desired exercise. After finding one just add how much time you spent exercising and the amount of sets and reps you did of it. Then watch your total time exercising update alongside your total sets and reps completed.

Log Your Route

Using the Geolocation API you can track your walk, run or bike ride wherever whenever. Just click the start button to start your activity. When finished you get to see your total time, pace, distance and calories burned. Then just click the save button to have your run be saved on the exercise page.
